.SAFETY & SECURITY

    At the Novotel New Delhi Aerocity, the safety and security of our guests are our first priority.

  • Emergency evacuation instructions are located at the back of your room door. It also indicates the location of the fire exits and hydrants on your floors.

  • The hotel is fully equipped with sprinklers, smoke detectors and other fire emergencies systems.

  • A safe deposit box is available in your room. We recommend you use it to store any valuables with your preferred code and not share it with anybody.

WHEN IN YOUR ROOM

.If someone knocks on the door, remember to look through your peep hole to identify the person before you open it. Please be advised that all our team members wear name badges.

If you do not wish to be disturbed, simply press the "Do Not Disturb" switch located near the room entrance.

WHEN YOU LEAVE YOUR ROOM:

Close your door and carry your room card with you. It is advisable not to hand over your key card to anybody else.

IN CASE OF EMERGENCY:

  • Keep calm and don't panic.

  • Take the nearest evacuation route assisted by the hotel personnel and re authorities. Be alert for public address announcements.

  • In the event of a major earthquake, protect your head from falling debris and stand underneath a strong structure like a structural beam or wall.

    • Walk calmly down to the evacuation site outside the building, once the tremors stop, as aftershocks are likely to occur.

WHEN MOVING AROUND THE AREA; IT IS ALWAYS ADVISABLE TO FOLLOW THE STEPS BELOW:

  • When using a taxi, take note of the taxi number.

  • Take a hotel card from Concierge.

  • Be alert of suspicious persons and activities.
